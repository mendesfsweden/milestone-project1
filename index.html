About Me

All my life I have been interested in different forms of art, such as photography, sculpture and painting. Four years ago, and after many life changes, I got the chance to study ceramic and I fell in love with it. Ever since then I never stopped. I have built my own atelier and that is where I produce all of my work.

Every single of my productions is 100% hand-made, which makes it unique.

I work in partnership with local restaurants but I can also make personalized pieces for private buyers. Have a look at what I do on the section bellow.

On the side, I also do small glazing work with tiles that will soon be available in the upcoming online store.
